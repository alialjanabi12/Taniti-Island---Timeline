Taniti Tourism

UX Design Timeline – 30-Day Plan

Days 1–2

Review survey results and "About the Island" document. Identify key user needs and pain points.

Days 3–4

Conduct competitive analysis of other tourism websites to gather design inspiration.

Day 5

Define project goals and success metrics. Create initial UX strategy.

Days 6–7

Develop a detailed user persona profile based on research.

Days 8–9

Sketch low-fidelity wireframes to outline navigation and layout.

Days 10–11

Create a digital version of the wireframes using Figma or Balsamiq.

Days 12–13

Develop content structure for pages (e.g., homepage, activities, lodging, booking, transportation).

Day 14

Conduct internal review and refine wireframes based on feedback.

Days 15–16

Conduct guerrilla usability testing with at least three users. Gather qualitative feedback.

Day 17

Analyze feedback, categorize actionable vs. non-actionable insights.

Days 18–19

Implement design adjustments based on usability test results.

Day 20

Begin creating an interactive prototype in VScode.

Day 21

Conduct an internal review of the prototype.

Days 22–23

Conduct a second round of usability testing on the interactive prototype.

Day 24

Implement final refinements based on testing insights.

Days 25–26

Create a document outlining usability tasks for Task 2 testing.

Days 27–28

Ensure the prototype meets all usability and accessibility standards.

Day 29

Final quality check and project review.

Day 30

Submit final project deliverables.
